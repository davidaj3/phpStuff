David A. Jones

I am a senior studying music composition at Brigham Young University-Idaho. I am from Lacey, Washington. Music has always been an invaluable part of my life--in many ways it defines who I am. I began playing music as early as age 8. My primary instrument is the French horn, though I played trumpet alongside the horn in high school, and I've dabbled in various percussion instruments. Inspired by the repertoire we performed in my high school's band program, I began composing my own works for band during my junior year, and I've been composing music for a variety of ensembles ever since. I served my mission in Manila, in the Philippines, and developed a great love for the people, the culture, and the language there. Following my graduation here at BYU-I, I plan to go on to study composition at the graduate level and earn my Master's and Doctorate in composition. Ultimately, I hope to teach music theory and composition at the college level while establishing my own career in composition.

Feel free to peruse my website. I've included some recordings of a few of my works. Feel free to give them a listen.
